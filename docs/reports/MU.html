Catalysts & what each lever is worth

  • Cost of capital: every 75bps of WACC moves fair value by ≈$2.26/share (7%). Rate path and the equity risk premium are the single biggest swing factor.
  • Terminal growth: 50bps of perpetuity growth is worth ≈$0.12/share — the market's read on the company's long-run competitive position.
  • Operating margin: each 100bps of EBIT margin across the forecast is worth ≈$2.50/share — watch pricing power, mix and cost discipline in quarterly prints.
  • Top-line delivery: our base assumes 6.7% year-one growth fading to 2.5%; sustained beats/misses versus that glide path are the cleanest re-rating trigger either way.

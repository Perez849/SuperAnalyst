Catalysts & what each lever is worth

  • Cost of capital: every 75bps of WACC moves fair value by ≈$23.87/share (21%). Rate path and the equity risk premium are the single biggest swing factor.
  • Terminal growth: 50bps of perpetuity growth is worth ≈$11.58/share — the market's read on the company's long-run competitive position.
  • Operating margin: each 100bps of EBIT margin across the forecast is worth ≈$2.85/share — watch pricing power, mix and cost discipline in quarterly prints.
  • Top-line delivery: our base assumes 25.0% year-one growth fading to 2.5%; sustained beats/misses versus that glide path are the cleanest re-rating trigger either way.

Key risks

  • Terminal value carries 86% of enterprise value — highly sensitive to perpetuity growth and WACC.
  • Through-cycle margin is an estimate; a structurally lower commodity price or cost inflation would reset the normalised earnings base.
  • Single third-party data source: restatements, non-recurring items or classification differences can shift the output.
